Megan: No, *you* hang up first.

Megan: No, *you* fucking hang up first!

Cueball: You hang up first, or we're *OVER!*

Megan: Then I guess we're fucking *OVER!*

Cueball: FINE!

Megan: ...

Cueball: ...

Megan: *You* move on and find somebody else first.
